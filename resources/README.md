## Planning-readiness checklist

The pack is ready for the portfolio-planning audit when:

- [ ] Required identity and contact fields are complete.
- [ ] All three projects have a summary, role, status, category, and case-study notes.
- [ ] Each project has one image in `cover/` and two to four visuals in `gallery/`.
- [ ] Live and repository links are supplied, marked not public / not applicable, or left as `TBD`.
- [ ] Five to eight useful website references include notes about what to borrow.
- [ ] Hero and About notes communicate who Arvind is and what he builds.
- [ ] The services form describes the work visitors should request.
- [ ] Staffly and Pro Shoot explain business-facing capabilities without implying client delivery or commercial proof.
- [ ] The real resale / inventory experience is presented as business context, not as a software project.

## Asset guidance

Use descriptive filenames such as `staffly-dashboard-desktop.webp`. Prefer clear, high-resolution PNG, JPG, or WebP files. Do not place sensitive or unlicensed material in this folder.
