From there I focus on information hierarchy, reusable frontend structure, maintainability, and a visual system that feels clean and intentional. I like building products that are practical first, then layering motion and polish where it adds clarity or personality.

I’m comfortable moving between dashboard-style learning builds, business and brand websites, creative portfolio experiences, and AI-oriented workflow ideas. That mix makes me think about structure, usability, storytelling, and implementation together instead of treating them as separate layers.
