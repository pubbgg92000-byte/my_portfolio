- **[Required] Supporting line:** I design and build modern product interfaces, internal tools, portfolio websites, and business-facing web experiences—combining frontend engineering, motion, and AI workflow thinking.

- **[Optional] Secondary action label:** Start a project

- **[Optional] Availability line:** Available for selected freelance builds, frontend product work, and AI workflow collaborations.

## Boundaries

- **Words or claims to avoid:**

  - “passionate coder”
  - “full-stack expert in everything”
  - “AI expert” unless the portfolio has enough shipped AI work to support it
  - generic buzzwords without proof
  - anything that makes the hero feel overloaded or vague

- **Visual or motion idea for the hero:** A premium dark hero with a strong headline on the left, a controlled visual system on the right, and subtle motion rather than heavy animation.

  Possible hero directions:

  - layered floating project previews / cards
  - a minimal 3D object or abstract visual tied to the portfolio theme
  - slow editorial text reveal + soft parallax
  - a “selected work / capabilities” side panel that hints at projects without cluttering the hero
